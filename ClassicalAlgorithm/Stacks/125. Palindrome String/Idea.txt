Find if given string is a palindrome or not using a stack.
Definition of palindrome: A palindrome is a sequence of characters that is same
backward or forward.
Eg. “AAABBBCCCBBBAAA”, “ABA” & “ABBA”
Hint: Push characters to the stack until the half-length of the string. Then popthese characters and then compare. Make sure you take care of the odd length
and even length.
